Our calculations are meant to be estimates and are based on averages. We'd love to be more specific if people request this.

Biking 14 mph for an hour = 600 calories, So 42 calories burned per mile.

Therefor, Miles per year = miles per day * 260 working days per year

US Requirement of 35.5 mpg so that's the average fuel efficiency I used.

Gallons used per year = Miles per year / 35 mpg

1 barrel of crude oil = 34.9 gallons since 1 barrel of US crude = 42 gallons of crude and 1 gallon of crude will produce 0.833 gallons of gas

barrels = gallons per year / 44gallons/barrel

Tree offset calculation is based on a tree planted in the humid tropics absorbing on average 50 pounds (22 kg) of carbon dioxide annually

Gasoline carbon content per gallon: 2.421 kgrams so Carbon per year = (gallons/year * 2.421 kgrams)

So

Trees = gallons/year *2.421 kg / 22kg/yr
